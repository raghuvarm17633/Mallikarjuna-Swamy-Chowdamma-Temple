Temple History

Established generations ago, this sacred temple stands as a testament to the enduring faith of the community. Built in traditional Dravidian style, the temple has been a spiritual sanctuary for thousands of devotees across the region.

🔱

Lord Mallikarjuna Swamy

Lord Mallikarjuna, an aspect of Shiva, is worshipped here with deep reverence. One of the 12 Jyotirlingas, his presence sanctifies this land. Devotees seek his blessings for strength, prosperity, and moksha.

🌸

Goddess Chowdamma

Goddess Chowdamma, the benevolent mother deity, is the presiding goddess of the village and surrounding areas. She is worshipped as a protector and fulfiller of devotees' wishes, especially during harvest seasons.

🪔

Temple Traditions

Ancient rituals passed down through generations of priests keep the spiritual flame alive. From Abhishekam to grand Jatara celebrations, every tradition carries the sacred wisdom of our ancestors.

Sacred Celebrations

Festivals

పండుగలు
🌾

Kolupulu

ANNUALLY (AS PER TELUGU CALENDAR)

A sacred village awakening festival where Goddess Chowdamma is ceremonially invoked and the deity is "awakened" with special rituals, offering of grains, and community prayers for prosperity and protection.

🔱

Maha Shivaratri

FEBRUARY / MARCH

The grandest celebration — an all-night vigil with Abhishekam every 3 hours, bhajans, and thousands of devotees seeking Shiva's blessings.

🪔

Karthika Masam

NOVEMBER / DECEMBER

The sacred month of Shiva. Special deepotsavams, lamp lighting, and daily Abhishekam throughout the entire month.

🌸

Ugadi

TELUGU NEW YEAR

Telugu New Year celebrations with special poojas, distribution of Ugadi pachadi, and blessings for a prosperous year ahead.

🐘

Vinayaka Chavithi

AUGUST / SEPTEMBER

Grand celebration of Lord Ganesha with procession, idol installation, cultural events, and community participation.

🎪

Annual Temple Jatara

ANNUALLY (AS PER CALENDAR)

The biggest community festival with cultural programs, processions, Goddess Chowdamma's special poojas, and devotees from across the region.

🏺

Bonalu

JULY / AUGUST

The beloved Telangana festival dedicated to Goddess Chowdamma. Women carry offerings on their heads in colorful processions.
